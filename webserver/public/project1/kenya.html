The smoky aroma of grilled meat that brings families together

üç¥ Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef or goat meat (cut into chunks)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

‚è±Ô∏è Prep: 15 min | üç≥ Cook: 20 min | ‚è≥ Total: 2h 35min

üë®‚Äçüç≥ Cooking Steps

  1. Marinate: In a large bowl, mix garlic, ginger, oil, salt, pepper, and paprika. Add meat and coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight for best flavor).
  2. Prepare Grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. If using charcoal, wait until the coals are white-hot.
  3. Grill: Thread meat onto skewers if desired. Grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until nicely charred on all sides.
  4. Finish: During the last few minutes of grilling, squeeze fresh lemon juice over the meat for extra flavor.
  5. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with ugali and kachumbari (tomato-onion salad).

Tip: For authentic flavor, use a charcoal grill and mop the meat with the marinade while grilling.
